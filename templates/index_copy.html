OUR MISSION

No matter what project we build, we all decided we wanted to give back to our communities and make an impact. Your Shelter hopes to make it more accessible for the homeless to find homeless shelters near them and get assistance for their needs. Your Shelter also provides a platform that enables volunteers to donate and register to programs to assist the homeless.
